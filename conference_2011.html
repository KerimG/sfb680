SFB 680 / FOR 1078 Joint Conference

Evolutionary Genomics: New Data, New Challenges

Time: September 29 (1:50pm) - October 1st (2:00pm) , 2011
Venue: Pelham (Chiemgau, Bavaria)

The aim of the meeting is to bring together a group of leading experts to discuss aspects of genome evolution that become accessible by using massive sequence and other molecular data.

Confirmed speakers include

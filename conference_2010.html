SFB 680 Conference

Molecular Basis of Evolutionary Innovations

Time: July 1 (2 pm) - July 3 (2:00 pm)
Venue: Marche-en-Famenne (Belgium)

This meeting will be hosted by SFB 680 to launch its second funding period. The aim of the meeting is to bring together a group of leading experts to discuss aspects of adaptive evolution: molecular mechanisms, functional consequences, inference from genomic and gene expression data, and population genetics.

Invited speakers include

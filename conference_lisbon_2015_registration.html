Forecasting Evolution? - Registration Page

Time: July 8 (2pm) - July 11 (1pm), 2015

The conference will be held at the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation in Lisbon. Due to the limited capacity of the conference hall, attendance will be by invitation only. The deadline for regular registrations has passed, and the meeting is currently fully booked. You can still register at this time by filling out the form below; we will notify you when places become available. Payment details will be sent with the confirmation of participation.

Accommodation: A number of rooms at a discounted conference rate (80 EUR per day) are available at hotels in the vicinity of the conference venue. Booking details will be sent with the confirmation of participation.

Conference fee (covers registration, welcome reception, conference dinner, lunches and coffee breaks during the conference)
Before April 30: Regular EUR 200, Student EUR 150
After April 30: Regular EUR 250, Student EUR 200

Travel grants: A limited number of travel grants is available to exceptionally qualified junior researchers with limited alternative funding. The deadline for travel grant applications has passed.

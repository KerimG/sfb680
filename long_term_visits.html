Long term visits

Support is available for a small number of long term research visits, e.g., sabbatical research projects. Such visits should be pre-arranged with a project leader of SFB 680. Applications should contain a CV, a concise description of the research project, and a cost summary, and should be sent to cstitz@uni-koeln.de.

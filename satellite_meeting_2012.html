Viral Evolution: Linking Genetics to Epidemics

Satellite Meeting of the Cologne Spring Meeting 2012: Molecular Ecology and Evolution

Time: February 24 - February 25 (2:00pm) , 2012. The morning session on February 24 is part of the Cologne Spring Meeting 2012.
Venue:
24.02.2012 Physics Institute, Zülpicher Str.77, Lecture Hall
25.02.2012 Biocenter, Zülpicher Str. 47b, Lecture Hall
Organizers: Michael Lässig (University of Cologne) and Thomas Lengauer (MPI Saarbruecken)

As its title suggests, the meeting aims at bringing together experts on genome evolution, population genetics, and epidemiology of viral systems to discuss emerging links between these fields.

Confirmed speakers include

Nico Beerenwinkel (Zürich)
